This article shows **`ArticleMap`** with **only a custom** `ViewLayer`: a GeoJSON polygon defined in `src/content/articles/shared/custom-demo-polygon-layer.tsx` (shared with other examples). Use this pattern when you author geometry and tooltips yourself instead of reusing built-in hazard or infrastructure layers.

For **hazard + infrastructure** layers from the app, see [Article map: built-in layers](/articles/20260328-article-map-demo).
